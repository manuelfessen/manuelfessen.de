"Rocket Surgery Made Easy" by Steve Krug is a must-read book for anyone who wants to improve their usability testing skills. 
It stresses the importance of testing designs to ensure they meet users' needs. The book provides practical tips on how to conduct usability tests with minimal resources and on how to analyze and interpret the results.
